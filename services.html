FEMA & Cross-Border Compliance Services

In an increasingly globalized business environment, cross-border transactions are subject to a complex framework of regulatory, secretarial, taxation, and reporting requirements. Non-compliance with the provisions of the Foreign Exchange Management Act, 1999 (FEMA) and related regulations can result in significant penalties and regulatory challenges.

We provide comprehensive advisory and compliance support to Indian companies, foreign investors, overseas entities, NRIs, and start-ups engaged in international business activities. Our services are designed to ensure seamless compliance with FEMA, Reserve Bank of India (RBI) regulations, the Companies Act, 2013, and allied laws.

Our Areas of Expertise

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)

  • Advisory on entry routes, sectoral caps, and investment structures.
  • Assistance in issue and transfer of shares to non-residents.
  • FEMA compliance for rights issues, bonus issues, ESOPs, and convertible instruments.
  • Filing and reporting of FDI transactions through the RBI reporting framework.
  • Compliance support relating to downstream investments.

Overseas Direct Investment (ODI)

  • Structuring and advisory for overseas acquisitions and investments.
  • Compliance under the Overseas Investment Rules and Regulations.

External Commercial Borrowings (ECB)

  • Advisory on ECB eligibility, borrowing limits, and end-use restrictions.
  • Assistance in documentation and reporting requirements.
  • Compliance monitoring throughout the tenure of the borrowing.
