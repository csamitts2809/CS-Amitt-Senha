Business Setup & Structuring

Establishing the right legal foundation is critical. We assist entrepreneurs and startups in establishing Private Limited Companies, Public Companies, One Person Companies (OPCs), and Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs). Our services cover incorporation, documentation, and post-incorporation compliance.

Compliance Retainership Services

Regulatory compliance is an ongoing responsibility. Our retainership services provide businesses with continuous professional support for corporate and secretarial matters. We maintain statutory records and ensure timely filings with regulatory authorities.

Due Diligence

Thorough verification is essential for informed decision-making. We conduct legal and secretarial due diligence for mergers, acquisitions, and fundraising. We focus on statutory compliance, governance practices, and potential regulatory risks.

Corporate Governance Advisory

Strong governance builds sustainable businesses. We assist boards and management teams in implementing ethical decision-making processes and transparent governance structures that align with global standards.

FEMA & Cross-Border Compliance

FEMA compliance is critical for globalized businesses. We provide comprehensive advisory on Foreign Direct Investment (FDI), Overseas Direct Investment (ODI), and reporting with the Reserve Bank of India (RBI).

We ensure seamless compliance for Indian companies, foreign investors, and NRIs engaged in international business.

FDI & ODI Advisory

  • Assistance in issue and transfer of shares to non-residents.
  • Filing FDI transactions through RBI reporting framework.
  • Structuring overseas acquisitions and investments.

Compliance & Reporting

  • ECB documentation and compliance monitoring.
  • FEMA due diligence for mergers and restructuring.
  • Filing of statutory returns with regulatory authorities.

International Tax Coordination

Coordination for tax-efficient structuring of cross-border transactions and DTAA advisory.
